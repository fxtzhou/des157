What intrigued you to Design & why did you choose the field you are in now?

What intrigued me with design is that it has the power to change the world. Design is not just about aesthetic pleasure, it can be redesigned to help people.

What have been your biggest struggles of your career?

Confidence in my designs and lack of time to execute passion projects.

What have your experiences been like as a person of color in the design industry? Does those experiences shape how you solve design-related problems?

As a first-generation Latina, I feel like I have been exposed to a lot of different obstacles in college, but those experiences have helped me become a more well-rounded person. The design industry can be competitive, but my experiences and background as sources of inspiration be have helped guide me with design-related problems.

How can design be more accommodating to underrepresented populations of people?

Because of underrepresentation, a lot of things in society were made by people not of color, and don't necessarily accommodate the needs to others. With design, they can be redesigned be more inclusive.
